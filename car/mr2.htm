History

I bought the car in June 1995 after having spent the last 12 years driving Kit Cars. The MR2 had 56,000 miles on the clock when I bought it, but had  covered over 208,000, when I sold it in May 2001, passing the 200,000 milestone over the Christmas 2000 Holidays. Those miles took me all over Scotland, including the Outer Hebrides and down to Austria & Switzerland via Belgium, Germany, France, Luxembourg, and Liechtenstein. I was sorry to part with it, but I have moved on to bigger things, namely a Landrover Discovery.

History

I bought the Landrover Discovery in May 2001 to replace my ageing MR2. The car originally had about 130,000 miles on it but is now only a couple of thousand miles short of 200,000. I have had to replace both the transfer box and steering box along with numerous bearings and seals. The car is pretty much standard with only minimal protection added to the underside. Diff guards both front and rear and a Terrain Master steering guard up front to provide both protection and recovery points. I don't have a electrical winch but carry a hand winch when venturing off road. Hella 300 Lamps on the front provide ample illumination in the dark.

In April 2004 I replaced the 200tdi which had covered in excess of 200,000 miles with a newer 300tdi ES, with 45,000 miles on the clock. The steering and diff guards have now been transferred over along with the spot lights.
